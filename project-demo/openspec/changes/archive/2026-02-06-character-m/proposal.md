# Proposal: Add Character M (The Mysterious Client)

## Goal
Introduce a new character, **M**, to the narrative. She serves as the inciting incident for Detective K's latest case.

## Context
The story requires a catalyst to pull K out of his routine. M represents the "Call to Adventure" in a noir/cyberpunk setting. She is a mysterious, alluring client who appears at midnight with a request that breaks the norm.

## Character Concept
- **Name**: M
- **Role**: Client / Femme Fatale
- **Archetype**: The Mysterious Stranger
- **Vibe**: Sexy, Elegant, Dangerous, Enigmatic.

## Requirements
1.  **Asset Creation**: Create `assets/characters/M.yaml`.
2.  **Visual Definition**:
    - **Appearance**: Strikingly beautiful, conveying both allure and hidden power.
    - **Style**: High-contrast to K's worn look. Sleek, expensive, perhaps concealing cybernetic enhancements.
3.  **Narrative Function**: She must bridge the gap between K's low-life reality and a high-stakes corporate or conspiracy plot.

## Impact
- **New Files**: `assets/characters/M.yaml`
- **Story**: Enables the "Midnight Visit" scene.
